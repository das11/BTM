Company Overview
Bodhi Tree Multimedia Pvt. Ltd. was incorporated in July 2013, is based in Mumbai. The production house has been playing an iconic role as it is perennially changing theperspective of Indian viewers by bringing forth a distinctive line-up of youth-based shows.
The company has produced a robust line up of 8 shows since nception across varied genres and broadcasters. It has an impressive slate of upcoming content pipeline.

Sukesh Motwani
Director
Sukesh functions as the Chief Creative Officer of the company in addition to being the promoter. His experience encompasses the entire gamut of the media business right from content production to broadcasting over a period of two decades. He has worked with the most leading media brands like Zee Television,UTV & Nimbus. His last held position was head of fiction programming in Zee TV.
Sukesh was also responsible for the launch of the Zee Youth GEC- Zee Next where he headed the content creation team. Sukesh has been involved in creation of more than 10,000 plus hours of content across all genres.
This includes close to 75 daily soaps in various capacities from conceptualising, writing, creative direction & commissioning.
