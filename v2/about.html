WHAT WE DO

The company has been at the forefront of creating differentiated & edgy content through its distinct lineup shows. It’s gamut of activities covers content production for television, films and digital platforms.

It has produced a robust line up more than 30 shows since its inception across most major Broadcasters & OTT platforms.

It has produced more than 1500+ hours of content on Television and OTT.

The shows have encompassed all genres - drama, thriller, horror, comedy and lifestyle.

The company has produced shows across multiple languages – Hindi, Tamil, Marathi, Gujarati and Bengali..

Senior Management
Mautik Tolia

Director

Mautik is the Chief Executive Officer of the company and the promoter. He has been associated with top media companies in India such as Neo Sports, UTV & Nimbus with close to 15 years of experience. His experience ranges in the areas of setting up various media ventures, writing, broadcasting, television content creation & production. His filmography includes more than 30+ television shows across various genres and television channels in various capacities.

Sukesh Motwani

Director

Sukesh is the founder Director of the company and also functions as its Chief Creative Officer of the company. His experience encompasses the entire gamut of the media business right from content production to broadcasting over a period of two decades. He has worked with leading media brands like Zee Television, UTV & Nimbus. His last held position was head of fiction programming at Zee TV. He was also responsible for the launch of the Zee Youth GEC – Zee Next where he headed the content creation team. Sukesh has been involved in creation of more than 10,000 plus hours of content across all genres. This includes close to 75 daily soaps in various capacities from ideation, concept development, story / screenplay supervision, creative direction & commissioning.

Ravi Bhavani Shankar Bhatt

CFO

Mr. Ravi Bhavani Shankar Bhatt, is the Chief Financial Officer (CFO).He holds a Graduation degree in Commerce from Mumbai university. He joined us in 2014 as Head of Accounts & Finance and in Aug 2020 was appointed as Chief Financial Officer (CFO). Prior to joining our Company, he was associated with Contiloe Pictures Pvt Ltd. He is a seasoned professional with 20+ years of experience.

Persis Signaporia

Creative Producer

Persis started her career with advertising, graduating to copy-group head in Grey Worldwide. She has worked with the top media companies – MTV, Cinevista, Balaji & DJ’s. Persis has set up more than 20 television shows in capacity of Creative Director including iconic shows such as 'Jassi Jaisi Koi Nahin', 'Parvarish', 'Left Right Left' to name a few. She was instrumental in pioneering the youth fiction genre having creatively produced shows such as 'Kitni Mast Hai Zindagi' for MTV and 'Pyar Vyaar And All That' for Cinevista. She also played an instrumental role in setting up the youth focused new media content division for Balaji.

Malavika Asthana

Script Head

Malavika works as Script Head on key projects. An extremely skilled and prolific writer, she has been a part of many daily shows. She has been the story writer for Nisha aur uske cousins and screenplay writer for various shows such as 'Aapki Antara', 'Jhansi Ki Rani’, 'Veer Shivaji', 'Hitler Didi' to name a few. She has also served as the Senior Executive Producer at SAB where she handled shows like Left Right Left and Mohalla Mohabbat Walla.
